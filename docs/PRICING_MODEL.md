## Pricing Strategy & Psychology

### Value-Based Pricing

**Why $99, $299, $699?**
- **$99:** Psychological threshold (under $100)
- **$299:** 3x jump encourages growth-mindset customers
- **$699:** 2.3x jump targets established brands
- Avoids awkward middle tier (clear separation)

**Anchoring:**
- Enterprise tier "custom pricing" makes Pro ($699) feel affordable
- DFY pricing ($5K-$25K) anchors SaaS subscriptions as a steal

**Tiered Value Prop:**
- Starter: "Get started"
- Growth: "Scale faster"
- Professional: "Automate everything"
- Enterprise: "Custom solutions"

---

### Pricing Experiments (Year 1)

**Test 1: Annual Discount**
- Offer 20% off for annual prepay
- Improves cash flow
- Reduces churn (commitment)

**Test 2: Usage-Based Pricing**
- Alternative model: $49/month base + $0.01 per AI call
- May appeal to low-volume users
- Compare vs. flat tiers

**Test 3: Freemium**
- Free tier: 1 store, 500 AI calls/month, limited features
- Converts to paid at 10-15% rate
- Increases top-of-funnel volume

**Test 4: Add-On Bundles**
- "Growth Pack": Extra 10K AI calls + 5K emails for $50/month
- Increase ARPU without forcing tier upgrades

## Upgrade & Downgrade Policies

### Upgrade Path
- **Immediate:** Upgrade takes effect immediately
- **Prorated:** Credit unused portion of current tier
- **Encouragement:** In-app prompts when hitting limits (90% usage)

### Downgrade Path
- **End of Billing Cycle:** Downgrade at renewal, not immediately
- **Retention Offer:** "Stay on Growth for $249 (save $50/mo)"
- **Feedback:** Ask why they're downgrading (improve product)

### Churn Prevention
- **At-risk alerts:** Usage dropped 50% ‚Üí Trigger email
- **Win-back campaigns:** Canceled users get 50% off to return
